Don't include offers to do demos or walkthroughs in messages drafted for the user.

**Why:** The user doesn't want to be volunteered for additional commitments they didn't agree to.

**How to apply:** When drafting team messages, keep them informational. Point people at the code/docs and let them self-serve. Don't add lines like "happy to walk through it" or "let me know if you want a demo".
